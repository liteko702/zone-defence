👾 ENEMIES

7 enemy types across 3 galaxies. Currently no introduction when new types appear — Sprint 2 will add briefing screens.

🛸

BOUNCE Patrol Drone

Blue disc with scanning beam. Bounces off walls randomly — predictable but fills the space.
⚡ Counter: Watch their angle and cut around them. Easy to avoid individually.
HP: 1 · Appears: Galaxy 0+
🔺

CHASE Hunter

Red interceptor with forward-swept wings. Actively chases the player — relentless and aggressive.
⚡ Counter: Keep moving. Use speed boosts. Lure away then cut behind them.
HP: 1 · Appears: Galaxy 0+
🐍

TRACER Serpent Drone

Green segmented worm with mandibles. Follows player's trail path — hunts along your cuts.
⚡ Counter: Keep cuts short. Don't retrace your path. Shoot from distance.
HP: 1 · Appears: Galaxy 1+
🛡️

SHIELD Armor Tank

Gold armoured gunship with shield energy field. Slow but takes 2 hits to destroy.
⚡ Counter: Use double-damage ships (Titan/Nova) or focus fire. Enclose in territory for instant kill.
HP: 2 · Appears: Galaxy 1+
🧬

SPLIT Biomech Entity

Pink blob with dashed division line and two nuclei. When killed, splits into 2 smaller bouncers.
⚡ Counter: Enclose them in territory (no split). Or clear space before killing to handle the spawns.
HP: 1 (spawns 2) · Appears: Galaxy 1+
💎

PHASE Crystal Ship

Purple translucent diamond with energy field. Phases in and out — completely invulnerable while phased (transparent).
⚡ Counter: Wait for them to become solid. Time your cuts for when they're phased out of your path.
HP: 1 · Appears: Galaxy 2+
🔘

ORBIT Ring Drone

Orange ring with spinning energy arcs and pulsing core. Moves in circular orbit patterns — creates denial zones.
⚡ Counter: Learn their orbit radius. Cut between orbits. Their path is predictable if you watch the pattern.
HP: 1 · Appears: Galaxy 2+

🚀 DEVELOPMENT ROADMAP

Ordered by priority. Each sprint is a focused chunk of work.

NEXT Sprint 1 — Restructure Levels

Reduce 109 → ~63 levels. Cap 5 per constellation. Make each level feel distinct.

  • Trim constellation star patterns to max 5 stars
  • Steeper difficulty jumps between levels
  • Rebalance enemy counts, speeds, target %

Sprint 2 — Enemy Introductions

"THREAT DETECTED" briefing screen when new enemy type first appears.

  • Show enemy name, sprite, behaviour, counter-strategy
  • Only shows once per save file
  • Skippable with tap

Sprint 3 — Boss Overhaul #1

Make boss fights epic. Unique music, intro sequence, multi-phase fights.

  • Unique boss music (fast electronic, building intensity)
  • Boss intro sequence — name card, warning siren
  • Multi-phase: behaviour changes at 50% and 25% HP
  • Boss health bar, attack patterns, death animation

Sprint 4 — Map Avatar & Navigation

Your ship appears on the map and flies between constellation nodes.

  • Player ship avatar on map (matches equipped ship)
  • Fly animation between nodes
  • More visual detail on constellation nodes
  • Level preview panel (enemies, target, bonus timer)

Sprint 5 — New Galaxies

Expand with more galaxies, each with new enemy types and mechanics.

Sprint 6 — Ship Upgrades & Visuals

Better ship sprites, upgrade trees, visual weapon differences.

Sprint 7 — Shop & Economy #2

Skins, trails, upgrades in the SHOP tab.

Sprint 8 — Polish & QoL

Tutorial, cinematics, PWA, bonus timer system.

Sprint 9 — Social & Replayability

Achievements, leaderboards, endless mode, daily challenges.
