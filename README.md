# Zone Defence

Qix-style territory capture game with multiple levels and progressive difficulty.

## How to Play
- Move around the perimeter of the game area
- Venture into unclaimed territory to draw lines
- Return to the border to claim all enclosed territory
- Avoid enemies — they kill you if they touch you or your trail
- Claim the target percentage to advance to the next level

## Levels
1. **The Basics** — One slow enemy (75%)
2. **Double Trouble** — Two enemies (75%)
3. **The Chaser** — Introduces chasing enemies (75%)
4. **Swarm** — Three bouncers + a chaser (80%)
5. **Speed Demon** — Fast enemies everywhere (80%)
6. **The Gauntlet** — Six enemies, two chasers (85%)
