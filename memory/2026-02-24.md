# Zone Defence — Session 2026-02-24

## Key Decisions
- **Terminology locked:** Universe (stage/world) → Constellation (playable level) → Round (life/attempt)
- **3 Universes × 4 Constellations = 12 levels** (expandable later)
  - Universe 1: Andromeda (pure zone capture, no shooting)
  - Universe 2: Milky Way (shooting unlocks, tracer/shield/split/orbit enemies)
  - Universe 3: Nebula Prime (all mechanics, hardest)
- **Boss fights** at end of each universe (constellation 4, 8, 12)
  - Boss mechanic: capture in zone → stun → shoot while stunned → rockets to dodge
  - Bosses: Cygnus Guardian (6 HP), Draco Wyrm (10 HP), Phoenix Lord (15 HP)
- **No shooting in Universe 1** — shooting unlocks from Universe 2 onwards
- **Enemies always respawn** to keep minimum 1 alive (zone capture stays core)
- **5 ships** in hangar: Viper (0★), Phantom (4★), Titan (10★), Pulse (18★), Nova (28★)
